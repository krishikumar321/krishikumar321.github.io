Projects & Research

Li-Polymer Battery Prototype (Nov 2025 – Present)

Personal project and Prototype Club initiative focused on developing a Li-polymer battery with an extended safe operating temperature range, targeting aerospace applications such as drones and satellites.

Work involves electrolyte optimization and electrode material selection to improve thermal stability while maintaining power density.

Phase-Field Modeling of Rapid Alloy Solidification (May – July 2025)

Research internship project at IISc Bengaluru. Developed a custom C++ phase-field simulation code based on Karma’s model to study microstructural evolution during rapid solidification.

Implemented finite difference methods for numerical discretization, demonstrating computational materials engineering proficiency.

Computational Tools for Experimental Analysis (March 2025)

Developed Python and C++ scripts to automate experimental data reduction and analysis.

  • Statistical analysis tool for sieve analysis data
  • Numerical solver for ordinary differential equations (ODEs)

Reclamation of Valuable Materials from War Waste (Oct 2024)

Case study project on recovery of Neodymium from NdFeB magnets using green metallurgy extraction techniques (GMETs), including economic analysis.
